About Me

Hi, I'm Zoltan! I do many different things, including software engineering, developing games, and cyber security analyst!

I'm a self-taught programmer from the UAE, currently living in the U.S. with a passion for learning new things and exploring. There have been many ups and downs in my career but I manage to make it stable again. I also have a strong belief in my faith, Islam. Currently studying to become a Mufti.
